Self Care



Physical Health

Physical health is the condition of our bodies. This can range from our outside physique to something we can't see such as any diseases or internal issues. Keeping healthy physically is important if we want to live a comfortable life. It helps to reduce the risk of diseases, strengthen our bones and muscles, and improve our brain health. Mental Health

Mental health includes our psychological, emotional and social well-being. Having a well balanced mental health is important because affects our everyday life. It affects how we look at the world. It influences our actions, thoughts, and feelings. To learn more about mental health, click here
