Decreased Mental and Physical Health

What Does that Look Like?

Often times, many of us neglect to properly care for our mental and physical well being. From a range of signs and symptoms, we are able to identify what specific conditions follow, regarding the decline in one's well-being. Let's navigate what those signs and conditions look like.

Signs and Symptoms

Listed are a few signs and symptoms to assist and identify, when someone's mental and physical state are being compromised. Some common signs and symptoms are:

  • Change in sleep or appetite
  • Change in mood
  • Withdrawal
  • Drop in functioning
  • Hyper Sensitivity
  • Apathy
  • Feeling detached/disconnected
  • Illogical thinking
  • Nervousness
  • Unusual behavior

Conditions

When our mental and physical state are compromised, many are not always fully aware of how that can potentially lead to illnesses. Some conditions can be minor, while others pose as life threatening. Some conditions are:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety Disorder
  • Dissociative Disorder
  • PTSD
  • Eating Disorders
  • Self-Harm
  • Substance Use Disorders
    • The over-consunption of legal/illegal drugs, alcohol, prescribed medication
  • Sleep Disorders
