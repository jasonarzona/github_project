Your Mental Health is Important

About 1 in 5 adults suffer and live with a mental illness



What is mental illness?

Mental illnesses, also known as mental disorders, is a condition that affects mood, thinking or behavior. Some examples of mental disorders are depression, anxiety, schizophrenia, eating or addictive disorders. They can also range from mild, moderate to severe.



Who does it affect?

Mental illnesses can affect literally anyone of any age, gender or race.

  • 49.5% of US adolescents suffer from a mental disorder
  • 21% of US adults suffer from a mental disorder
  • 51.2% of females received mental health services
  • 37.4% of males received mental health services


How to take care of your mental health

The best thing you can do is to see a licensed profession but if that is not possible then here are some things you can do to help take care of your mental health

  • Talk with someone you trust
  • Write about your feelings in a diary
  • Eat meals and drink water regularly
  • Get enough sleep
  • Try physical activities
  • Do things to clear your mind that you enjoy
  • Go for a walk
  • Play video games

If these don't help and you still feel like you need help, don't be afraid to call someone at the suicide prevention hotline at 800-273-8255
